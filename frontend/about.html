How CanIEdit works

A simple pipeline so you always know what happens to your files.

1. Upload

Drag and drop

Files go to a short-lived, isolated bucket with encryption in transit.

2. Process

FastAPI workers

CPU-optimized tasks handle merges, conversions, and cleanups in parallel queues.

3. Clean

Auto-delete

Completed jobs trigger cleanup routines that remove temporary files within minutes.
