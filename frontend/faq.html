FAQs

Answers about CanIEdit tools, pricing, and privacy

Everything you need to know about file limits, storage, support, billing, and what is on the roadmap.

General

Core questions about using the tools.

Is the PDF merger free?+−

Yes. Merge unlimited PDFs without logins, watermarks, or trials.

What file types are supported?+−

PDF, PNG, JPG, WEBP, HEIC, DOCX, PPTX, and XLSX are covered today. More formats ship as new tools launch.

Do I need an account?+−

No sign-in is required for free tools. Paid tiers use email to manage limits, support, and billing.

Privacy & security

How we handle files and data.

Are my documents secure?+−

Uploads are encrypted in transit and stored in isolated buckets. Cleanup jobs remove temporary files within minutes.

Do you keep copies of my files?+−

We keep files only long enough to process and deliver results. Paid plans can request custom retention windows.

Do you use third-party trackers?+−

We keep analytics minimal and do not place tracking pixels on processed files. See the privacy policy for details.

Limits & performance

Usage caps and what to expect.

What are the file size limits?+−

Free: 25 MB per file. Creator: 250 MB. Studio: 2 GB with resumable uploads. Business: 5 GB and regional storage options.

How many edits can I run?+−

Free: 20 edits/day. Creator: 200/day with background queue. Studio and Business: unlimited with batching and automation.

How fast do jobs finish?+−

Most tasks complete in seconds. Studio and Business use priority queues and parallel workers for heavy jobs.

Billing & plans

Payments, upgrades, and trials.

Can I switch plans later?+−

Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime. Prorating is automatic.

Is there a free trial?+−

Creator and Studio include a 7-day trial. Business pilots can be scoped with onboarding support.

Do you offer enterprise options?+−

Yes. Business includes SSO, SCIM, audit logs, custom retention, and private endpoints. Contact us to scope requirements.

Roadmap & support

Upcoming tools and getting help.
